Welcome!

This site hosts automatically formatted rotation charts of the cards from Love Live SIF All Stars. These charts can be useful for determining at a glance which of the girls is the most likely to receive another card next, based on how many cards are remaining in the current rotation and how long it has been since their last one.

Certainly the developers are not bound to rigid rotations, a fact most evident in event card rotations but frequently seen in other categories of cards as well. So this site should be used as an aid for educated guesses, not a singular source of truth. They will do whatever they please, count on that.

Data is retrieved from All Stars Card Database. They do not guarantee full validity of the data, therefore a similar disclaimer applies to this site. Errors may be present but a best effort is made.

Card statistics page uses the original JP release dates for calculating how long it has been since a card was added to the game.

Getting started

Just navigate to any of the categories in the navigation bar.

Note that you can also operate the settings by keybindings:

  • Q Toggles idolized switch.
  • W Toggles reversed order switch.
  • S Cycles to the next source highlight option. Use with the Shift key to go backwards.
  • R Resets everything back to default.
  • Number keys Changes the active sub-page when applicable.
